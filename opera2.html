CAPT-

Farewell, my own, Light of my life, farewell! For crime unknown I go to a dungeon cell.

SIR JOSEPH-

A bone, a bone I'll pick with this sailor fell; Let him be shown at once At once to his dungeon cell.

JOS-

I will atone. In the meantime farewell! And all alone Rejoice in your dungeon cell!
